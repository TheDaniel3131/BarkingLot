Our Mission

Our BARKING LOT Mission is Clear:

To save the most vulnerable dogs, give them a second chance, and enrich the lives of dogs and humans.

This simple statement is our barking lot zone, guiding every initiative to ensure we have the greatest impact on people's protection of dogs.

In carrying out this mission, we continually return to our core values of innovation, advocacy, opportunity, compassion, and excellence. These are not just words for us, but reminders. If we are to continue to change the future of animal welfare, we must innovate and find creative solutions. We must regularly do online and offline outreach activities. We must give dogs a second chance. We must have compassion because that is the foundation of a better world. We must always strive for excellence because our pets and our communities deserve our best.
