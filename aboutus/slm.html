Explore Our Barking Lot Mission:

Barking Lot is a self-funded, not-for-profit rescue organisation based in Malaysia. We strive to provide a second opportunity for animals who find themselves in the situation of being surrendered, homeless, neglected or abused. Barking Lot's mission is to provide wildlife rescue and rehabilitation therapy, as well as awareness programmes on animal care and adoptions for homeless and abandoned animals.
